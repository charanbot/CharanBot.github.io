Chicken Sandwich Recipe

1/17/2021

Step 1: Smear the butter on the bread slice and toast them.

Step 2: Add some oil and saute garlic untill aromatic.

Step 3: Add the Chicken and add some salt and saute it.

Step 4: Add the spices and some red chilli powder.

Step 5: Add Mayo, pepper powder, onions, corriander leaves and mix well.

Step 6: Add sauted chicken and mix well.

Step 7:Spread it on a bread slice and cover it with an another slice.

Step 8: Enjoy
